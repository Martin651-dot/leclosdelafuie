# Prompt Claude Design — Site Le Clos de la Fuie

---

## Prompt à coller dans Claude Design

---

Create a vacation rental website for **Le Clos de la Fuie**, a charming family house in the Perche region of Normandy, France, 2 hours from Paris.

**Reference site to draw inspiration from:** https://leclosdelafuie.fr/
Keep what works: the warm personal tone, the nature-forward visuals, the regional identity. Improve everything that prevents bookings.

---

### About the property

- **Name:** Le Clos de la Fuie
- **Location:** La Perrière, Perche, Normandie — 2h from Paris
- **Capacity:** 6 people, 3 bedrooms
- **Key features:** Large family garden, garden cabin, game room, authentic Norman architecture, countryside views
- **Vibe:** Authentic, peaceful, family-friendly. Not luxury — real, lived-in, welcoming.
- **Host:** Aurélie, local tourism guide, knows the region deeply

---

### Target audience

Parisian and international families (4–6 people) looking for a full week escape in nature. They book 2–6 months in advance, compare multiple properties, and need to feel confident before committing.

---

### Primary goal of the website

**Convert visitors into booking requests.** Every design decision should serve this. The main CTA ("Réserver" / "Check availability") must be visible at all times — in the hero, in the nav, and after every key section.

---

### Pages and sections to include

**Homepage**
- Full-width hero with the best exterior photo + headline that sells the feeling ("Une semaine au vert, à 2h de Paris") + primary CTA button
- Quick property summary (6 personnes · 3 chambres · Perche · À partir de X€/semaine)
- Photo gallery highlight (3–4 best shots: exterior, living room, garden, cabin)
- 3 key selling points with icons (Ex: "Jardin privatif", "Cabane pour les enfants", "2h de Paris")
- Guest reviews section (star rating + 2–3 quotes)
- Sticky booking CTA banner

**La Maison (The House)**
- Room-by-room description with photos: bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living spaces
- Full equipment list (WiFi, linen included, parking, etc.)
- Floorplan or room layout if available

**Le Jardin & La Cabane (Garden & Cabin)**
- Dedicated section — this is a key differentiator for families with children
- Photos of the garden, cabin, outdoor seating

**Réserver (Book)**
- Pricing table: weekly rates by season (low / mid / high)
- Availability calendar (or link to Airbnb/Booking)
- What's included / not included
- Booking process explained in 3 simple steps
- Cancellation policy
- Contact form as fallback

**Le Territoire (The Region)**
- What to do nearby: walks, markets, châteaux, restaurants
- Map of the Perche region
- Aurélie's personal recommendations as local guide

**Avis Clients (Reviews)**
- Full page of guest reviews with names and dates
- Average rating prominently displayed

**FAQ**
- Arrival/departure times
- Pets allowed?
- Minimum stay
- Parking
- Baby equipment available?

---

### Design direction

- **Mood:** Warm, natural, honest. Think linen, stone, green fields. Not sterile or overly polished.
- **Color palette:** Warm whites, sage green, warm beige/sand, dark forest green for CTAs
- **Typography:** Serif for headings (elegant, timeless), clean sans-serif for body
- **Photography style:** Natural light, real family moments, green landscapes — not stock photos
- **Mobile-first:** Most visitors will land from Instagram or a Google search on their phone
- **Language:** French primary, with an optional English version for international guests

---

### Critical improvements over the current site

1. **Pricing must be visible** — the current site shows no rates; visitors leave to compare elsewhere
2. **CTA must be persistent** — a "Réserver" button always visible, not buried in the nav
3. **Social proof above the fold** — show the star rating immediately, don't make visitors hunt for reviews
4. **Availability signal** — even a simple "Été 2026 : quelques semaines disponibles" creates urgency
5. **Room photos** — current site lacks detailed interior shots; families need to see every bedroom
6. **Trust signals** — host photo, Superhost badge if applicable, years of experience, number of stays
